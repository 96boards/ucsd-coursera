Contacts

The mailto: links on this page will work if your email client is properly registered as a helper application for your browser. If you haven't linked your email client to your browser, just send your comments to the email address given. We may contact you to request additional information. Please understand that we are unable to reply to all comments individually because of the volume of email we receive.

Feature requests

We welcome suggestions for new features or enhancements of existing features in LightWAVE.

Please check LightWAVE's to-do list to see if your proposed feature is already planned. If it isn't, send an email with the subject line Feature request to lightwave@physionet.org, and tell us about the feature you would like to see in a future version of LightWAVE.

Bug reports

Please check LightWAVE's bug list to see if the bug you found is already known. If it isn't, send an email with the subject line Bug report to lightwave@physionet.org, and tell us about it. Please include this information in your report:

  • LightWAVE client and server version numbers (see the Settings tab)
  • Browser name and version number
  • Operating system and version number
  • Database and record names (see the Choose input tab)
  • What you did that triggered the bug
  • What you observed that was unexpected
  • What you expected to observe
  • Is the unexpected behavior reproducible?

Questions

Please have a look at the FAQ. If you haven't found an answer there, send your question by email to lightwave@physionet.org. We give priority in answering questions to those who have read the FAQ. How can we tell who has done so? That's easy; we look for the magic word in the subject line of the email.
